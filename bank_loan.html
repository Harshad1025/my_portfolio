This project focuses on analyzing loan data to distinguish between good and bad loans using key performance indicators (KPIs). It includes metrics such as application percentages, funded amounts, and total received amounts for both good and bad loans. The purpose is to provide a comprehensive overview of lending operations through a loan status grid view, visualizations depicting monthly trends, regional analysis, loan term breakdowns, and more.
