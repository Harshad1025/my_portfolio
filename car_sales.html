This project aims to create a comprehensive Car Sales Dashboard in Power BI, visualizing critical KPIs to enhance sales performance analysis. It addresses the need for real-time insights into key metrics such as total sales, average prices, and cars sold, enabling informed decision-making and trend identification. The dashboard includes various chart types like line charts, pie charts, and map charts to represent sales trends, distribution across body styles and colors, and geographic sales distribution. Additionally, detailed grids provide comprehensive information on individual car sales, facilitating thorough analysis and data-driven decision-making. Problem Statement
