T This case study delved into Udemy's schema and Entity-Relationship diagram, highlighting the platform's structure and functionality. Udemy revolutionizes online learning with a diverse course selection and global instructor-learner connections. The schema's entities, such as users, courses, instructors, and enrollments, illustrate its comprehensive framework. Understanding this schema underscores Udemy's effective management of its educational ecosystem, fostering accessible and engaging learning experiences. This well-designed schema enables Udemy to empower individuals in achieving their learning goals.
